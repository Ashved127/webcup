About Aquamagic Ltd

The Mauritius Aquarium welcomes you to a unique experience of relaxation and discovery! Situated on the North West of Mauritius, in the charming small village of Pointe Aux Piments, the Mauritius Aquarium offers a unique experience. A leisurely stroll through the five well laid out principal buildings surrounded by a tropical garden, allows its visitors to discover the rich aquatic life which inhabits the coastal and pelagic waters of Mauritius.

OUR CREATURES

Moorish idol (Pavillon cocher)

White to pale yellow body with two broad vertical black bands. Very pointed snout.

Giant trevally

Flattened body, with a somewhat bulging head. Dark yellow to silver above, silvery below.

Clown Fish

Orange with three white bands on the head and body. The white bands are outlined in black.

Yellow Fin Tuna Fish

The Yellow Fin Tuna is probably the most colorful of all the tunas.

Queen Angelfish

A marine angelfish commonly found near reefs in the warmer sections of the western Atlantic Ocean..

Sea Turtle

Sea turtles, sometimes called marine turtles, are reptiles of the order Testudines.
